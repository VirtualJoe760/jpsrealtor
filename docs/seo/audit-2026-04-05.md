## 9. Key Takeaway

The **performance fix (CLS)** was the biggest win since the last audit — the site went from a 56 to 89 Lighthouse performance score in 2 days. However, the **sitemap remains broken** which is the #1 bottleneck preventing Google from discovering the 5,000+ pages on the site. Until the sitemap is properly indexing URLs, the 293 city pages and hundreds of neighborhood pages will remain invisible. After sitemap, **backlink building** is the next highest-leverage activity — the domain has virtually zero authority and can't compete even for low-volume local queries.
