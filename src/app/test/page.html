    48 @ Baristo, Palm Springs, CA

    The 48@Baristo community was built in 2005 and features two and three bedroom units which range in size from 1,475 sq. ft. to 1,766 sq. ft. All units come with private garages, walled courtyard, as well as pool & spas. There is a 30 day minimum rental rule at 48@Baristo.
