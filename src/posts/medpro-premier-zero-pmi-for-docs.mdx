## CMG MedPro Premier Landing

**Zero PMI, up to 100% financing for medical pros.**

✅ Up to 100% LTV with **no mortgage insurance** – borrowers never pay PMI. ✅ Loan amounts up to **$2,000,000** for primary residences. ✅ Qualify on a **signed employment contract**; start date must be within 150 days of closing. ✅ Student loans in deferment or IBR can be **excluded from DTI** for residents and fellows. ✅ Minimum LTV **90.01%** – designed for buyers with limited down payment.
