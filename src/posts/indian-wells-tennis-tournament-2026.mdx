# Indian Wells Tennis Tournament 2026: Dates, Tickets & Local Guide

---

![Welcome to the tennis gardens](https://res.cloudinary.com/duqgao9h8/image/upload/v1741196933/bnp-4.jpg)

---

The **Indian Wells Tennis Tournament**, officially the **BNP Paribas Open**, returns in 2026 as one of the premier tennis events in the world. Often called the *"Fifth Grand Slam,"* this tournament draws top ATP and WTA players to the Coachella Valley every March. Here's your complete guide to this year's event.

## Dates & Location

The 2026 BNP Paribas Open takes place in **March 2026** at the **Indian Wells Tennis Garden**, located at 78-200 Miles Avenue, Indian Wells, CA 92210. The venue features Stadium 1 (the second-largest tennis stadium in the world), along with multiple show courts and practice facilities set against a stunning desert mountain backdrop.

## Tickets & Entry

Tickets range from **general admission grounds passes** to **premium stadium seating** and **VIP hospitality packages**. VIP options include exclusive seating, hospitality suites, and behind-the-scenes access. Sessions sell out quickly — purchase tickets early through the official BNP Paribas Open website.

## Top Players to Watch

---

![Tennis Match](https://res.cloudinary.com/duqgao9h8/image/upload/v1741196933/bnp-2.jpg)

---

The tournament consistently attracts the world's best players. Recent champions and top-ranked stars including **Carlos Alcaraz, Jannik Sinner, Iga Swiatek, and Aryna Sabalenka** headline the draw. Early rounds offer the best opportunity to watch emerging talent up close before the stadium rounds heat up.

## Fan Experience

Beyond the matches, the BNP Paribas Open offers:

- **Practice Court Viewing** — Watch top players warm up just feet away
- **Live Music & Entertainment** — Fan zones with interactive experiences
- **Dining** — On-site restaurants including **Nobu** for world-class sushi
- **Shopping** — Official tournament merchandise and local vendors
- **Autograph Sessions** — Meet players and get signed memorabilia

## Where to Stay

---

![Enjoying the Tennis Tournament](https://res.cloudinary.com/duqgao9h8/image/upload/v1741196933/bnp-3.jpg)

---

Indian Wells and surrounding cities offer accommodations for every budget:

- **Luxury**: Hyatt Regency Indian Wells, Miramonte Resort, Renaissance Esmeralda
- **Mid-Range**: Hotels in Palm Desert and La Quinta are 10-15 minutes from the venue
- **Short-Term Rentals**: Airbnb and VRBO properties near Mountain Cove and the Tennis Garden are popular for week-long stays during the tournament

## Where to Eat

The Coachella Valley dining scene shines during tournament season:

- **The Pink Cabana** — Mediterranean flavors at the Saguaro
- **LG's Prime Steakhouse** — Classic American steak in Palm Desert
- **Bucatini Trattoria** — Authentic Italian on El Paseo
- **Nobu** — Japanese cuisine right at the stadium
- **Pacifica Seafood** — Fresh seafood on El Paseo

## Tips for Attendees

- **Arrive Early** — Parking fills up fast, especially for evening sessions
- **Check the Weather** — March temperatures range from 70-85°F; bring sunscreen and a hat
- **Explore the Area** — Visit [Joshua Tree National Park](https://www.nps.gov/jotr/), the El Paseo shopping district, or take a drive through the scenic desert communities
- **Plan Your Stay** — Book accommodations well in advance; tournament week is peak season
